I am a B.Sc. Computer Science student at [The Chinese University of Hong Kong](https://www.cuhk.edu.hk/), driven by a deep interest in _Programming Languages_, particularly _Type Systems_ and _Language Features_.

I am currently undertaking research under the supervision of Prof. Lauren Pick on the _Autoformalisation of Programming Language Theorems_ as my Bachelor's thesis, whilst also collaborating on a project focused on specification generation to support agent-driven code synthesis.

During an academic exchange at [KTH Royal Institute of Technology](https://www.kth.se/), I joined the HScala research group under Prof. Philipp Haller. Our current work involves designing a type system for heterogeneous [Scala](https://www.scala-lang.org/) programming across native CPU and GPU runtimes, along with developing the initial compiler prototype.
